Muffin Information

Famous people who have eaten muffins

  • US President Barrack Obama: During his inaugural speech, Obama said, "This country needs change, but first, I'm going to eat this blueberry muffin."
  • Sherlock Holmes: In the classic story, "Sherlock Holmes and the Case of Who Stole my Muffin Please Give it Back", Sherlock ate a bran muffin at the climax of the story.
